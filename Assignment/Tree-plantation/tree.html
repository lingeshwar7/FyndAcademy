Why concern yourself with tree plantation?

Trees clean the air and help you breathe by sequestering carbon dioxide

Are good for your mental and physical health

Help fight erosion and desertification as unprotected earth would deteriorate under the sun losing fertility

Provide a habitat and a relief to endangered species

Areas for Tree Plantation

There are many areas around the world where deforestation has occurred, leaving vast tracts of land unused and barren. These areas could be ideal for tree plantation, as they are often located in regions that are already experiencing the effects of climate change, such as increased desertification, loss of biodiversity, and decreased soil fertility.

By planting trees in these areas, we can help to restore the natural ecosystems that were once present and create new habitats for wildlife. Trees also absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, which can help to mitigate the effects of climate change and improve air quality.
